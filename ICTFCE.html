The current pandemic situation has been posing new challenges to the educational eco-system worldwide. Several governments across the world have temporarily closed all the educational institutions. Around the globe, there is an increasing demand for online education.Main objectives of ICTFCE-2020 is to bring together teachers, students, researchers, edtech companies, policymakers in education on a common platform to discuss on the CURRENT TRENDS AND FUTURE CHALLENGES IN EDUCATION
